A Salad (that is probably not unlimited in quantity)

Description

This is some salad to eat when your doctor says you should make better food choices.

You can also just eat it if you enjoy eating salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Hard Boiled Egg
  • Red Onions
  • Sauce to put on your salad (commonly referred to as "Salad Dressing")
  • Basil (FRESH ONLY)

Steps

  1. Chop up all ingredients into sizes that you can eat
  2. Combine all ingredients into a bowl
  3. Mix all ingredients
  4. Put an amount of salad into a smaller bowl
  5. Eat the salad from the smaller bowl

    6. If any of this is confusing, clearly you do not have the wisdom of Odin and should consider staying out of the kitchen.
