Garfield's Favorite Lasagna

Description

This is some lasgna that you would probably eat.

Or possibly feed to a large, orange cat.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Nona's Love
  • Oregano
  • Basil (FRESH ONLY)

Steps

  1. Heat up your Sauce
  2. Boil your pasta sheets
  3. Spread sauce in an Auflauf pan
  4. Layer pasta sheet, cheese, and sauce until you have filled your pan
  5. Cover in foil
  6. Bake in a hot oven till done.

    7. If any of this is confusing, clearly you do not have the wisdom of Odin and should consider staying out of the kitchen.
