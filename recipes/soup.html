It's Some Soup

Description

This is some soup to warm your insides and stave off the cold.

It is most certainly not "water-based cooking".

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Fennel
  • Potatoes
  • Basil (FRESH ONLY)

Steps

  1. Heat up your broth
  2. Chop all of your other ingredients
  3. Put all chopped ingredients in the hot broth
  4. Cook until the ingredients are soft enough to eat.
  5. Pour into a bowl and eat.

    6. If any of this is confusing, clearly you do not have the wisdom of Odin and should consider staying out of the kitchen.
